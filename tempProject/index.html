About

eNSUT is an initiative by Aksh Bansal, Aniket Saini and Daya Singh to meet the academic needs of all newcomers to NSUT. The three of us leveraged our experience and knowledge that we gained during our struggle and we formulated this website while keeping in mind the challenges that we faced in our freshman year. This website consists of all the important and valuable information that is relevant to new NSUTians.
